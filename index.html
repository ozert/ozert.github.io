About Me

I am a passionate and experienced Data Scientist with extensive expertise in Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and MLOps, currently pursuing an MSc in Data Informatics, while working full-time in one of the leading companies in defense industry. With a robust technical foundation and hands-on experience, I excel at developing end-to-end solutions, enhancing team productivity, and creating innovative AI models for real-world applications. My technical expertise spans:

  • Developing AI solutions for IoT data
  • Anomaly detection in network traffic and IoT domains
  • Predictive maintenance
  • Creating AI-based search engines
  • Computer Vision
  • Large Language Models (LLM)
I thrive in dynamic environments where I can leverage my skills to drive innovation and deliver impactful results. Balancing full-time work and graduate studies, I am fully committed to continuous learning and growth, staying at the forefront of technological advancements to solve complex challenges and contribute effectively to any team. As a dedicated team player, AI researcher, developer, and maker, I also volunteer for Robotel, a social responsibility project. I seek to leverage my expertise to drive data-driven decision-making and contribute to cutting-edge projects in a dynamic organization.
